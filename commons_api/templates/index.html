Exposing political data from Wikidata

This site demonstrates data about politicians and political representation around the world as modelled in Wikidata by Wikiproject Every Politician. It was produced in pursuit of the Democratic Commons.

What's in here

At the moment, information about legislatures, constituencies, legislators and party affiliations, both current and historical.
